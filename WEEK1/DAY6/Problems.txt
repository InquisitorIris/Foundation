Day 6 — Weekend Practice (10 Practice + 10 Pattern = 20 Problems)

Practice Problems (10):

1. Find sum of squares of first N numbers.
2. Find product of digits of N.
3. Find smallest digit in N.
4. Find largest digit in N.
5. Check if N is strong number (sum of factorials of digits = N).
6. Find power (a^b) using loop.
7. Display multiplication tables 1–10.
8. Count positive, negative and zeros in a list of N numbers.
9. Compute average of entered numbers until user enters 0.
10. Find HCF and LCM for array of numbers.

Pattern Problems (10):

11. Right triangle of stars.
12. Inverted triangle of stars.
13. Full pyramid of stars.
14. Number triangle (1 2 3…).
15. Floyd’s triangle.
16. Hollow square.
17. Diamond pattern.
18. Pascal’s triangle (up to N rows).
19. Numeric pyramid (1, 22, 333…).
20. Hourglass pattern.
